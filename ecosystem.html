IIT Delhi Startup Ecosystem

Explore the incubators, startup communities, funding bodies and innovation centers that support entrepreneurs at IIT Delhi.

Ecosystem Organizations

🚀 Entrepreneurship Development Cell

The primary entrepreneurship community at IIT Delhi. Organizes workshops, competitions, startup clinics and networking opportunities.

🏢 FITT

Foundation for Innovation and Technology Transfer. Supports startups through mentorship, industry partnerships and incubation.

💡 SInC

Student Incubation Cell helping student founders build and launch ventures.

🔬 IHFC

Technology Innovation Hub focused on deep-tech startups, funding opportunities and advanced research commercialization.

⚙️ Makerspace

Rapid prototyping facilities, fabrication tools and hardware development support.

📜 i-TTO

Technology transfer office supporting patents, licensing and intellectual property commercialization.

Founder Roadmap Through IIT Delhi

💡 Idea Stage

Attend eDC workshops, hackathons and startup events to discover opportunities.

🔍 Validation

Use mentor sessions and startup clinics to validate your assumptions.

🛠 MVP

Build prototypes using Makerspace resources and technical communities.

💰 Funding

📈 Scale

Connect with investors, accelerators and alumni founders.
